    Published on Oct 29, 2012

    I want to hear from you! For chip-to-chip communication, what type of tutorial would you like to see? I will do a tutorial of the most popular idea, but this idea must only use components from the previous tutorials.

    In this video, I use two chips and establish one way communication using the TX pin of the transmitting chip and the RX pin of the receiving chip. The control is produced by a button and LEDs are used to test the button's functionality and the show the successful communication at the receiving end.
